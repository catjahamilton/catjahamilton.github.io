About

Catja first became involved in Lighting Design while studying English at Cambridge University, where she was the Lighting Designer and Technician for over 20 shows. These included ‘Lagoon’: The Cambridge Footlights International Tour Show 2016, which toured the UK, US, and Grand Cayman; the Cambridge European Theatre Group’s production of ‘Hamlet’, which toured to Switzerland and Belgium; a two-week run of ‘West Side Story’ at the ADC Theatre; and five productions at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival.

Since graduating from Cambridge, Catja has continued to pursue theatrical lighting, working as the Associate Lighting Designer for ‘Maklena’ (Camden People’s Theatre, touring) under Rory Beaton. She has also been the Lighting Designer for several projects, including ‘Wife’ at the Jermyn Street Theatre and the professional debut of ‘ERIS’ at the Bunker Theatre.
